== Description ==
 
Transform your WooCommerce 'My Account' page with our Easy My Account Customizer! This plugin enhances the user experience by offering flexible and stylish design options. It's perfect for store owners looking to personalize and streamline their site.

<h3>Features:</h3>

- <b>Menu Position Customization:</b> Position your menu on the left, right, or horizontally.
- <b>Three Design Styles:</b> Choose from simple, modern, or dark themes to suit your store's style.
- <b>User-Friendly Interface:</b> Easy to use, making customization accessible for all.
- <b>Responsive Design:</b> Ensures a consistent user experience on all devices.
- <b>Seamless Integration:</b> Complements existing WooCommerce setups without hassle.
- <b>Regular Updates:</b> Benefit from ongoing enhancements and bug fixes.

Elevate your WooCommerce store with just a few clicks. Download now for a more engaging customer experience!

== Installation ==

1. Login in your WordPress admin panel.   
2. Navigate to Plugins => Add New from left menu bar.
3. Now search "WooCommerce my account customize" in search box.
4. Click on Install Now button
5. Then activate the plugin.
6. Woocommerce my account > Settings > Save your Settings.
7. Check Frontend My Account page.
